About Plants

Hydrangea Shrub

The Endless Summer Hydrangea is one of the most popular hydrangea. This plant blooms from early summer through Thanksgiving in most growing regions.

  • Thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it especially versatile.
  • Doesn't require excessive pruning to thrive.
  • Able to bloom from early summer through the late fall.
Rose Shrub

One of the longest blooming periods available, from early summer to late fall.

  • This cultivar is more resistant to black spot, rust, and mildew than any other variety.
  • It's also resistant to pests, making harsh sprays unnecessary.
  • This improved variety will even grow in the shade, giving you more areas to plant.
Evergreen Trees

Evergreen Trees stay green year-round and won't drop their leaves, despite the weather. Trees that only keep their foliage year-round in warmer climates are also referred to as Evergreen Trees since they're meant for specific growing zones.

  • Cold hardy
  • Adaptable to a variety of soil conditions
  • It blocks noise, neighbors, and unsightly views
